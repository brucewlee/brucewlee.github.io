I'm an undergraduate freshman at the University of Pennsylvania pursuing dual degree in Computer Engineering and Physics. I'm also in the [Vagelos Integrated Program in Energy Research (VIPER)](https://www.viper.upenn.edu/people/bruce-lee), a specialized degree program between the College of Arts and Sciences and Penn Engineering.

I'm broadly interested in natural language processing and machine learning. I'm currently working at <a href="https://www.lxper.com/">LXPER AI</a>, researching explainable readability assessment. I also interned at the <a href="https://capp.ibs.re.kr/html/capp_en/">Center for Axion and Precision Physics Research (CAPP/IBS)</a> at KAIST, mainly working on cryogenic temperature measurement and superconducting quantum interference device (SQUID).

If you’re interested in getting in contact with me, please email me at brucelws@seas.upenn.edu.
