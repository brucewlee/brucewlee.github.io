Understanding the Metrics

Watts

Watts measure power output - the rate at which energy is being used. In rowing, it's a direct measure of how hard you're working. Higher watts mean more power per stroke and generally faster splits.

Stroke Rate

Measured in strokes per minute (spm), stroke rate indicates how many complete stoke cycles you do per minute.
