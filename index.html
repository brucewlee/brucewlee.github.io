I'm currently a junior at the University of Pennsylvania. I do AI research, but I don't have a specific direction yet. I do this work because I think it will eventually help expand human possibilities and productivity for generations.

In my personal life, I'm an athlete on my college rowing team and served in the Republic of Korea Marine Corps for two years. Penn lightweight rowing is one of the top programs in the league, and it's a big part of my college life.

Previously, I interned with IBM Research, NAVER Cloud, and the Center for Axion and Precision Physics Research. If you're interested in my work, I recommend checking out my published papers directly. My blog posts are more off-the-cuff.
