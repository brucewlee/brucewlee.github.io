I'm working on making intelligent systems smarter and understanding the nature of intelligence itself. The goal here is to augment general human capabilities while contributing to our collective knowledge.

I'm particular about good tooling in research. Some of my open-source libraries are now used in several research labs. Outside of research, I row for my college varsity team and served in the Marine Corps.
